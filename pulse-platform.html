One Engine. Eight Domains. Endless Room to Grow.

Every Pulse — FarmerPulse, GovPulse, HealthPulse, EduPulse, BusinessPulse, CommunityPulse, ResearchPulse, and EnterprisePulse — runs on the same capture, memory, and intelligence engine, with its own domain vocabulary, workflows, and dashboards layered on top.

Agriculture & agribusiness

FarmerPulse

FarmerPulse is CII's most mature Pulse — the Intent Exchange that turns WhatsApp conversations into verified buyer requests, seller listings, service opportunities, and real-time market intelligence across agricultural value chains.

  • Buy/sell intents matched automatically by commodity, location, and quantity
  • Finance, transport, storage, and processing requests structured the same way
  • Live market intelligence dashboard fed entirely by real conversations

Built on

Government & public service

GovPulse

GovPulse structures citizen conversations — requests, complaints, and feedback — into a queryable record that government departments can act on and report against, without asking citizens to fill in a single form.

  • Every citizen message becomes a tracked, categorized case
  • Departments see live dashboards of demand by service and region
  • Feedback loops directly into program and policy design

Health systems & NGOs

HealthPulse

HealthPulse turns patient and community-health conversations into structured signal — symptom reports, appointment requests, and outbreak-relevant patterns — surfaced to health workers and public-health teams in real time.

  • Structured symptom and service-request capture from community chat
  • Early-warning pattern detection across geographies
  • Built to respect strict privacy and consent boundaries by design

Built for sensitivity

HealthPulse applies the strictest visibility defaults in the Pulse ecosystem — health conversations are private unless a person or authorized health worker explicitly shares them.

Consent-first DesignRole-based AccessAudit Logging

Education & training

EduPulse

EduPulse structures learner and educator conversations into progress signals, support requests, and gaps in available training — connecting learners to the right training providers automatically.

  • Learner questions become structured support tickets and FAQ data
  • Institutions see where content or instruction gaps recur
  • Training-provider matching mirrors FarmerPulse's intent-matching model

SMEs & enterprise

BusinessPulse

BusinessPulse turns customer and supplier chat into structured leads, orders, and service intelligence — giving growing businesses a CRM that fills itself from real conversations instead of manual entry.

  • Inbound customer chats become structured leads and orders automatically
  • Supplier conversations feed procurement and inventory intelligence
  • Works alongside existing tools via API, not as a replacement

Built for growth

BusinessPulse is designed to onboard in days on a single WhatsApp Business number, then expand into web chat and Telegram as the business scales.
